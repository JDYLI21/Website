About us...

We were first introduced to waterways problems in New Zealand back in 1996, when we found out a lot of New Zealand's waterways, particularly the ones in Auckland, are in very bad shape. 62% of the rivers and lakes in Auckland are graded as poor by the Ministry for the Environment for swimming, as well as having no rivers graded as good.

We plan on stopping this, and restore New Zealand's pride as a clean and safe country whom one has freedom to do whatever they want, anywhere they want, safely.

We hope you join us on our journey.
